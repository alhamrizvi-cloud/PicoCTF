
Instead of using jadx, I just unzipped the APK and spent quite a bit of time exploring it manually. Eventually, I found a Base32-encoded string inside `./layout/activity_main.xml`.

We basically had the flag right there lol.

I decoded it using:

```bash
echo "OBUWG32DKRDHWMLUL53TI43OG5PWQNDSMRPXK3TSGR3DG3BRNY4V65DIGNPW2MDCGFWDGX3DGBSDG7I=" | base32 -d
```

and got the flag.
